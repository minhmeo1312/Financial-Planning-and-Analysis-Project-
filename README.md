# Financial-Planning-and-Analysis-Project-
FP&amp;A portfolio: 3-statement modeling (HubSpot SaaS, $2.6B rev), SMB financial planning (BrewCo, break-even &amp; scenario analysis), and management reporting with variance analysis (TechFlow). Includes DCF valuation, rolling forecasts, KPI dashboards, and executive commentary. Built in Excel.

FP&A Portfolio — Minh Hoang
Finance teams drown in spreadsheets but starve for insight. These projects solve three problems I see companies face every day.

01 — HubSpot SaaS FP&A Model
Problem: Boards want forward-looking projections, not last quarter's numbers.
Full 3-statement model (IS/CF/BS) with DCF valuation, 3-scenario toggle, rolling forecast, and SaaS KPIs (ARR, NRR, CAC, LTV). 25 worksheets, BS auto-balanced, audit trail included.
→ Base Case: $2.67B revenue · 61.8% EBITDA margin · Cash positive all scenarios

02 — BrewCo Small Business Financial Model
Problem: "Can I afford to open another location?" — and no model to test it.
24-month P&L, cash flow with shortfall alerts, break-even sensitivity table, and 3-scenario expansion analysis. No macros, Google Sheets compatible, built for non-finance users.
→ Base Case: 66% gross margin · 163 break-even customers/day · 10% safety margin

03 — TechFlow Management Reporting & Variance Analysis
Problem: Monthly reporting takes a week when it should take a day.
Budget vs Actual P&L with auto-calculated variances, KPI traffic lights, and executive commentary templates. 390 formulas, zero errors — repeatable monthly in under 2 hours.
→ FY Revenue: $32.3M · EBITDA variance: +$174K favorable
